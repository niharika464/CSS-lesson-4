The history of Homo sapiens spans approximately 300,000 years, beginning in Africa where our species first emerged. Over tens of thousands of years, early humans developed tools, art, and complex social structures. The migration out of Africa, starting around 70,000 years ago, led to the colonization of various continents. The advent of agriculture around 10,000 years ago marked a significant turning point, enabling the rise of civilizations. Over centuries, humans have shaped the world through technological, cultural, and social advancements, leading to the diverse societies we see today.
